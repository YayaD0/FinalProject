What is Mental Health?

🧠 Mental health falls under emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It is important because it plays a big part in the way one think, feel, and act. Day to day life with mental health issues can affect the way one handle things and make choices. Mental health can affect anyone from kids to adults. It is important that information about mental health is out there so that it is possible to detect or help someone in need.

What Causes Mental Health?

🧠 The cause of mental health is different for everyone not one certain thing causes one to develop a mental health problem. Some of the most known causes of mental health are life experiences such as trauma, biological factors, or family history of mental health.

Mental Wellness

🧠 Being positive plays a big role in mental wellness for everyone it allows one to realize there self worth, better deal with stress, and be productive. There are different ways to maintain your mental health which includes getting help if needed, communicating with others, being positive, and staying healthy. Although these are a few ways to maintain your mental health they are important ones which goes a long way for anyone in need.
